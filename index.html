Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make good pixel art with AI?

Keep your descriptions clear and concise. For example, instead of "a dog", try "cute golden retriever puppy with a red collar, retro arcade style". Select the pixel size (32px, 64px, or 128px) that best matches your vision.

Is this AI Pixel Art Generator free?

Yes, PixelArtGenerator is completely free to use. You can generate, customize, and download high-resolution PNG images without creating an account.
